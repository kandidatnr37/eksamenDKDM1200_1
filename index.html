Organic Farm Cooperative

The Organic Farm Cooperative brings together regional family farms committed to organic farming. Together we provide healthy, organic produce to the local community.

Where to buy

You can purchase your fresh, organic produce on various markets throughout the region, directly on the farm or have it delivered to your home or office.
Check-out the sections below for more information.


At the farm

Visiting a farm is more than just shopping. It is an experience for the whole family. Meet our farmers, learn about their work and produce. Some of our farmers have set opening hours, some even host their own events, others sell by appointment only. Please make sure to find out if your prefered farm is drop-by or by-appointment only!

Markets

Our farmers participate in farmers markets and other events throughout the entire region, some host their own events and others deliver to food-halls. Check-out this section to learn where to find our farmers' produce.

Shop online

A few of our farmers have started to take online orders. Your options vary from standard "pick-and-choose" to pre-packed boxes, with our without recipies and meal ideas. In most cases the farms offer a delivery service as well, some offer pick-up solutions. Click on the picture above to explore your opportunities.
